XIncProc XPointer Processor

The XPointer processor of XIncProc Framework implements XPointer Framework.

It supports the followings XPointer Schemes:

  • element(): full support
  • xmlns(): full support
  • xpath(): full support
  • xpointer(): partial support (only xpath part)
